Elements Interactive was founded in the Netherlands in 1996 as an interactive studio specialized in designing and developing applications for web and mobile. We are an enthusiastic and experienced team of over 35 professionals who share a passion for technology and a “can do” attitude.
To expand our operations, we have opened an office in Barcelona, Spain in October 2013. For the Barcelona office we are currently setting up a mobile development team.
We are looking for a Senior Android Developer (M/F) with a passion for building mobile apps. As Senior Android Developer you work in a team of professionals and implement rock solid solutions to create high quality mobile applications. As a domain specialist on Android, you are the go-to person for team members with questions on the platform and related technologies.
