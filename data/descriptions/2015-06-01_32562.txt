
Responsibilities:
The Graphic Designer is expected to work with the creative ideas and contribute with its own, supporting the  team. The main responsibilities of the role are the delivery of high quality work within schedule, to work closely with the  team to deliver creative materials, add value and knowledge to the delivery and execution of digital campaigns and to develop strong concepts from creative brief through to finished artwork. 
Requirements
Fully proficient in English and Spanish. Other languages are a plus.
Experience managing Retail and Fashion social media platforms. 
Outstanding Adobe CS skills (Photoshop/Illustrator/InDesign/Acrobat).
Strong working knowledge of digital design.
Proven track record in pre-press management.
Creative and flexible approach to projects and able to forge strong working relationships.
Skills in Flash and Illustrator would be an advantage.
Good typographic layout skills.
Ability to work on multiple short term tasks at once and prioritizing workloads.
Ability to work quickly from concept creation to finished product.
Excellent attention to detail, and able to work to tight deadlines. 
Candidate must live in Ecuador.
