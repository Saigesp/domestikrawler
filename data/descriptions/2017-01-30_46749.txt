MISSION
The Mid Market Creative team is responsible for designing dynamic banner and email campaign ads, and performing maintenance tasks to meet Criteo client expectations and to optimize their performance. Within the Creative team you’ll be responsible for performing creative design requests for Northern Europe clients (UK, Benelux and Nordics), according to the process and the related SLA. You will also be tasked with ensuring client advertiser success through daily design maintenance and improvements of ad and email campaigns.  This role is more about quality and production, than stand-alone creative design.  And this person MUST have a strong background in HTML for the design of banner/email campaigns.
