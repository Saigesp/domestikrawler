
An Experimental Space for Internet Professionals to Create, Connect, and Collaborate.
Welcome to Wiki People, an experimental space for internet professionals interested in collaborating on the development of online projects. This groups has a bootstrapping, hands on approach.
Whether you can participate on the our barcamps, weekend workshops, or at your own pace ... the idea is to collaborate and work on the projects you find more meaningful. Your contribution to the project will be measured based on hours dedicated and expertise provided.
Please visit http://www.meetup.com/Wiki-People/
