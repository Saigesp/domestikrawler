To expand our operations, we have opened an office in Barcelona, Spain in October 2013. For the Barcelona office we are currently setting up a new web development team.
We are looking for a Senior Python Django Developer (M/F) with a passion for building next-generation web applications. As a domain specialist on Python Django, you are the go-to person for team members with questions on the platform and related technologies.
