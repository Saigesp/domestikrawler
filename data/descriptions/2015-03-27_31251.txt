
Key responsibilities as a Junior Account Manager, under the guidance of a Senior Account Director:
•Support to the Senior Account Director on executing the account management.
•Help on producing and delivering against an agreed account plan and budget.
•Prepare the materials, presentations and looking for information needed for the client's projects.
•To ensure the client receives the best in class service.
•Manage suppliers briefs, timings and budget.
•Project status and deadline controlling
Skills and Attributes:
•Business English is a must (native or proficiency level)
•Outstanding communication skills, with a hands-on, friendly attitude
•Strong Excel and Power Point skills
•Excellent time-management skills, with the ability to effectively prioritize workload
•Used to work under pressure
•Entrepreneur instinct. Versatile and capable of learning constantly and developing new skills
•Communication skills to properly brief the creative team
