
-- Role Overview
As a Web Frontend Developer​ you will join the core engineering team of Mobile Jazz. You will
work closely together with other web developers, as well as with UX experts and graphic
designers on delivering excellent consulting work for our international clients.
-- Requirements
● Fluent in English.
● Feel comfortable writing JS as well as having experience working with modern frameworks like AngularJS or React.
● Have a solid understanding of SQL and have good working knowledge of MySql.
● Feel comfortable using git and the command line, and understand the benefits of working in a continuous integration / deployment environment.
● Personal projects that show an aptitude for engineering and product sense.
Bonus Skills and experience
● Front end - you have worked with SASS and have experience working with Compass, Bootstrap, Foundation or other CSS frameworks.
● Back end - you have in-depth experience with PHP and object-oriented programming.
