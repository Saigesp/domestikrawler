Our Recruitment Process
We aim to reply to all applicants within a couple weeks of receiving your application. Our interview process consists of several stages, each one allowing us get to know you better, professionally and technically. It is also an opportunity for you to gain a better understanding of our culture and the work we do here at Bloombees. The stages include:
- CV and source code repository code / public portfolio review
- The Code Test - We sometimes skip this if your portfolio of work has enough examples of code you’ve worked on to give us confidence in your ability to write code to solve a real-world problem.
- Phone / Skype Interview
- Face to Face interview
