
At BMAT we love music and technology! We analyze, index, and recognize all the music in the world to provide search, recommendation and monitoring solutions to the music industry. BMAT is a rapidly growing and passionate company, with a creative, multicultural and dynamic team which operates globally, working with more than 50 customers worldwide. Our clients include companies like Yamaha, EMI, Sony, Samsung, and Singtel.
Job Summary
The Head of Recordings will be responsible for set ups and maintenance of all recording machines worldwide (radio and TV), managing the team to assist clients and providers on a daily basis. He/She will provide creative solutions for logistic and technical necessities and will guarantee the quality of our services in all recording related issues.  
Essential Duties & Responsibilities
•	Be the ultimate responsible of the recordings set up process
•	Lead and manage the team to investigate and solve any questions and recordings related incidences
•	Coordinate and direct the Recordings department communication with providers and customers
•	Manage outsourcing and stock
•	Manage department budget and guarantee deliveries ensuring the accomplishment of deadlines
•	Advice on technical decisions
•	Run and prioritize tasks according to and report to the COO
•	Translate the Board strategy into quarter and weekly plans for the Recordings Department
