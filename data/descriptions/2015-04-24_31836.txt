
Job description: 
Reports to the Animation Director. The main responsibility is to work in close collaboration with Animators, Rigger Supervisor and Animation Director in order to produce high-quality animations.
Tasks and Responsibilities:
• Work with highest level animation shots.
• Control the quality and technic/tools used for animators on each scene.
• Ensure a nomenclature that is in keeping with project norms and the selection sets in the master scenes in order to facilitate the work;
• Ensure that your final result (animations) meets the artistic standards of the project;
Minimum requirements: 
• Experience: more than 7 years with, at least, one 3D animated feature film experience.
• Very good knowledge of Maya.
• Very good team building and communication.
• High level of English language (oral & written).
Others: 
• Knowledge of Shotgun.
