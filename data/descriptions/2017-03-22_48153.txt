
This position is full-time and based in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. (No freelancer)
Join an international team of designers who are passionate about branding and creative communication. As the Art Director, you will work closely with the team to research, conceptualize, design and develop consistent strategies for brands to become successful on the marketplace. We are a group of 18 in-house professionals with a strong marketing approach, specialized in digital, print, packaging and architecture which runs from B2B to mass market.

•	Work with external partners in production and execution
•	Be a hands-on maker and crafter to bring all your ideas to life
•	Contribute to strategic decisions, working with the design, product and executive teams
•	Lead design projects with internal and external resources
•	Give and solicit real time feedback from the team to continually raise the quality and craftsmanship of projects
