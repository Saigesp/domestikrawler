
Company Overview
With unique Luxury &amp; Character Hotels in over 130 countries worldwide, Splendia.com is the brand of choice for the most discerning of travellers.
Based in offices in Barcelona, Miami and Hong Kong, the Splendia team is young, dynamic and motivated. A staff of 80 people originating from countries all over the world and speaking a multitude of languages, they collectively make a diverse team of travel expertise.
Working within the Content team in our Barcelona office, your role will be to assist the Picture Manager in processing pictures for Splendia.
Tasks
You will play a key role in selecting and processing pictures for the Splendia website – carrying out the complete process, from original photography to final publication. This includes:
-	The selection of pictures that are in line with the Splendia luxury brand image
-	Digital processing and optimization of pictures using Adobe Photoshop for online publication (retouching and resizing)
-	Uploading pictures using an in-house picture management system
-	Following company procedures for naming and storing pictures
Requirements
-	An undergraduate with the ability to understand our luxury brand concept
-	A keen eye for design and photography
-	Good knowledge of picture retouching in Photoshop (colour, brightness, contrast…)
-	Organised with attention to detail
-	Enthusiasm and initiative
