
New Relic
Senior Front End Engineer - CSS/JavaScript/HTML
Barcelona, Spain

At New Relic, we provide our customers with instant and deep insight into their application’s behavior and performance. With our SaaS-based application performance monitoring (APM) solution, we have our customer’s backs by transparently instrumenting their application and reporting issues, often before the customer knows that there is a problem. We give our customers as much information as possible to help them tune their site’s reliability and performance.
We are looking for an experienced Senior Front End Engineer to join our growing team. You will be involved in the entire technology stack and will be responsible for building single page Javascript web applications with complex and detailed User Interfaces. This role will also require you to be involved with technical design, and ultimately help shape the complex UI/UX.
As a Software Engineer at New Relic, you will work with some of the best engineers and tackle some of the most complex problems in the industry and as a member of Barcelona team you will help shape and grow the 1st New Relic European development center.
The right candidate likely has: 

* Extensive experience building cross-browser, responsive, and scalable HTML & CSS
* Experience working with modular CSS architectures (SUIT, BEM, SMACSS, OOCSS...)
* Experience in working with the current Front-end scene, CSS pre/post-processors, build systems, module loaders, and dependency management.
* Expertise in JavaScript, DOM and HTML5 APIs
* Demonstrable ability to write complex MVC JavaScript applications. Our frontend is about 80% JavaScript!
* Design focus with strong attention to details and a strong understanding of UI and UX principles
* Practice with design software such as Fireworks, Sketch, Illustrator.
* Great team player skills
* Ability to speak fluent English
* Authorized to work in Spain (EEA, http://europa.eu/about-eu/countries/ )
Bonus points for experience with Python/Django, being an Open source contributor, speaking Spanish and to master drifting skills at MK8.
