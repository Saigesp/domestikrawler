Position Summary:

This internship position offers the scope for learning not only about email marketing processes and strategy but also to gain a basic knowledge of online marketing in the hotel industry. 
Job Description:
• Organizing and maintaining client email lists
• Analyze and generate reports for campaign results
• Assisting with migrating clients to our new email system
• Depending on experience, you will also be involved in Email design 
• Coding the designs to HTML/system requirements 
• Testing and deployment of campaigns
Requirements: 
• Being a university student in order to have an internship agreement, Good level of English, written and spoken  
• Knowledge of Microsoft Office Suite (Primarily Outlook, Word and if possible competent user of Excel).
• Basic knowledge of HTML coding and Photoshop 
• Knowledge of Google Analytics (desired but not required)
• Must have excellent communication skills.
