
At Euromillions.com we are looking for a new talent for our development team.
What are we looking for?
Somebody that enjoys programming and is always eager to learn and improve.
Some keywords: Zend, Phalcon, Vagrant, Docker, Ansible, Node.js, Jenkins… 
We don’t want a guru nor an solitary ninja developer focused on the new trendy language. 
We want team players that are versatile enough, but with a main expertise in Php and Javascript.
We want somebody that agrees with the Agile Manifesto, and works accordingly.
