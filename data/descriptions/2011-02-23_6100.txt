
PARFOIS is selecting a Creative Director to take Head of the Design Team in their headquarters in O Porto (Portugal).
Descripción: 
The designer will take Head of the Design Team of PARFOIS and will be responsible for the organization and development of various fashion accessories collections in each season, in cooperation with the team of buyers.
Functions:
• Investigate the trends that will define the collections of the following season
• Development: define shapes, colors and textiles
• Coordinate the work of the designers
Requisitos mínimos: 
- We are looking for professionals that have Fashion Design Training (or other studies related with this area)
- 5 years of working experience has a Fashion Designer and leading teams.
- To know well and be curious about fashion trends is vital.
- Being fluent in the English language is essential and being available to travel is imperative.
- Ability to lead and having a team working spirit is essential.
