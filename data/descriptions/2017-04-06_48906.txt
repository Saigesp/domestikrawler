
Job Description
Responsibilities
• Create believable high quality animations, taking direct part of their implementation into the game;
• Get actively involved in the game creation process by interacting with the game designer and art director;
• Contribute effectively to the art pipeline and target platform constraints;
• Meet project deadlines/milestones as set by project leads.
Qualifications
Requirements
• Passion for videogames;
• Demonstrate fluency in:
-Animation principles
- Character Acting
- Character anatomy
- Physics-based motion
- Able to handle styles from cartoony to realistic
• Understanding of video game boundaries for animation;
• Solid background in academic drawing, sketching and/or illustration;
• Proved understanding of animation 3D packages (3D Studio Max, Maya)
• Experience with Motion Capture editing
Assets
• Experience in game industry –ideally several shipped titles;
• Rigging and skinning skills;
• Specific experience with 3D Studio Max Biped
• Experience with animation networks of commercial game engines;
• Cinematographic background knowledge (cameras, editing, storytelling…);
• Fluent level of English
