The day to day activities:
As a Junior Test Engineer you will have experience delivering quality assurance in a web/mobile environment, delivering quality code that conforms to our high standards.

We're looking for a person passionate about testing, someone that holds up to date views concerning the state-of-the-art in the testing world and promotes new ideas and frameworks within the wider technology team.

- 1 year of experience testing websites and/or mobile applications
- A people person, sharing ideas with others and inquisitive by nature
- Enthusiastic about learning – reading, testing books and blogs and attending testing conferences
- Knowledge in modern testing methodologies, practices and approaches (Waterfall, Agile – - Scrum/XP/Lean/Kanban, RAD..), TDD and BDD will be a plus.
