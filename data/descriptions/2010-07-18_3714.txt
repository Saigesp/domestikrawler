
Gameloft is a leading international publisher and developer of video games for mobile phones, smart phones and downloadable games for consoles. Established in 1999, it has emerged as one of the top innovators in its field and is recognized by its peers and its clients for the quality of its games.
Following its strategy of constantly delivering top games to players worldwide, Gameloft is now opening a new creation studio in Madrid, Spain. 
In order to create the successes of tomorrow, Gameloft Madrid is hiring: 
GAME DESIGNERS – SPORTS / iPhone-iPad &amp;amp;amp; mobile phones
Responsibilities:
Game designers offer their teams creative propositions in design and implementation of gameplay mechanics and features. Their main responsibilities are:
-	Benchmarking: Documentation and analyzing abilities;
-	Game design of all game mechanics and features, from the pitch to the final version of the game;
-	Intelligent behavior design (AI for team sports, notably), using general game concepts to create inspired and original environments;
-	Team coordination: Coordinating graphics and development teams assigned to the project with the producer.
Requirements:
-	Huge sports fan
-	Expertise in game systems for all types of sports (individual sports, team sports, recreational sports, etc.);
-	Able to convey ideas concisely and as part of a detailed analysis;
-	High visual creativity and passion for creating video games, particularly sports simulations; 
-	Leadership, motivation and ability to communicate as part of the project development team;
-	Passion for sports and sports news;
-	Knowledge of classic console video games, their flagship titles and their audience;
-	Candidates must have advanced knowledge of programming languages (object-oriented and natural language programming);
-	Able to listen to feedback from other people and constantly critique your work to achieve the best possible quality;
-	Able to illustrate and/or prototype your ideas visually using standard industry production tools (Photoshop, Flash, Sketch-up, 3D Studio Max, Unity, Blitz, etc.);
-	Excellent fluency in English.
-	Must live or be able to work in Madrid, Spain.
Assets:
-	Good knowledge and practical experience in producing 3D animations and implementation tools (editors, etc.)
-	Good general knowledge (History, literature, science, entertainment) and a sense of aesthetics for creating general style guides
-	Marketing knowledge: Analyzing and documenting market trends and ability to define and target a specific audience
-	Experience in video games, notably Nintendo DS and/or Wii projects, an asset 
-	Experience in sports game design a major asset
Benefits:
-	Opportunity to work in a newly created studio and learn in a professional environment with people who have a passion for creating video games;
-	Open working climate with young, dynamic teams from around the world
-	Salary based upon experience and merit;
-	Promotion possibilities: Senior Designer, Lead Designer
-	Permanent contract, full-time job and additional merit pay system
-	We are offering relocation support for candidates willing to move to Madrid
