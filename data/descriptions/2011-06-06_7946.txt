
Role Summary
We are seeking an experienced in-house Digital Designer with a genuine love for all things online. The Senior Designer will be responsible for all creative direction of the company, including Branding, Website Design, Email Design, Banner Ads, Print etc. It is vital the Senior Digital Designer has amazing layout skills, typography that will blow our minds and lots of experience at digital agencies at a top level. The Senior Digital Designer's portfolio needs to be packed with examples of credible design work for corporate clients and creative briefs alike. They will be required to ensure stunning, accessible and efficient work that will encourage more site visits, more click-throughs and subsequently more sales by enriching user engagement and ensuring the best user experience.
The ideal candidate will be passionate about web design with a finger on the pulse of current design trends. They will need to be open minded and excited about the possibility of Digital Design for a variety of audiences, alongside a passion for craft and beautiful design. A key part of the role will be to help grow and lead an excellent team of passionate and lively junior designers. 
The Senior Digital Designer will be joining BaseKit at an exciting time as we launch the product across the world (recently Spain, Mexico, Brazil, Peru, Argentina, Chile). The first task for this role will be to re-design the BaseKit brand, ensuring that we have the creative behind our big plans for the future.

BaseKit is a well-funded early stage company that is changing the way websites are created. BaseKit delivers a web-based software as a service (SaaS) platform enabling the creation of dynamic, rich and functional websites &amp; web applications quickly &amp; easily - without the necessity to code. 
There are more than 100m small businesses in the world.
