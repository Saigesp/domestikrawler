
Hola somos Eyeblaster 
Nuestra oficina de Madrid busca analista programador web que lidere el departamento de soluciones a soporte. Es decir, serás la persona técnica de contacto en Eyeblaster para todos los soportes publicitarios digitales en los que se sirven nuestros anuncios.
Generarás código, supervisarás el del soporte y resolverás incidencias técnicas. Propondrás mejoras y soluciones y darás apoyo.
Será responsable de comunicarte tanto con soportes externos como con equipos internos locales e internacionales. Recibirás peticiones por mail y teléfono que resolverás directamente o escalarás a los grupos necesarios para su resolución.

•	To analyze different types of code (i.e. HTML, XML, CSS, JavaScript) required
•	Identify types of ads (i.e. Image, Flash, Text/Content, Rich Media, Video, Search, etc.) required
•	Troubleshoot, locate, and resolve ad delivery problems autonomously in order to reduce escalations to the management team
•	Ability to analyze, research, troubleshoot, reproduce, and debug delivery of search, contextual, display, and rich media (Flash) advertisements
•	Identify and understand nuances between various popular web browsers and operating systems as they relate to online advertising and delivery
•	Explain technical concepts to technical and non-technical staff and customers in an effective and respectful manner
•	Successfully deal with ambiguity and excel at identifying ambiguous problems and ad platforms
•	Excellent written and oral communication skills
•	Ability to work well and excel in a team environment and with moderate supervision
